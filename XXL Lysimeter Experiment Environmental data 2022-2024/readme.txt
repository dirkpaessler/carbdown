
This contains the following data (usually one file per year):

Rain: Data from MeteoRain 200 Compact rain sensor showing rain/time and total rain
Weather: Data from MeteoHelix IoT Pro and MeteoWind IoT Pro weather station

Soil Moisture: Data from 30 and 60 cm depth using electronic soil sensors (Dragino Soil EC sensors)

Soil Temperature: Data from 30 cm depth using electronic soil sensors (Dragino Soil EC sensors)

Please note that between May 2022 and September 2022 we also had artifical irrigation on the lysimeters, but not later.
